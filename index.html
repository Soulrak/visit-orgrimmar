Visit Orgrimmar

With its towering walls, bustling marketplaces, and fierce inhabitants, this ancient city is the heart of the Horde's power and a hub of activity for adventurers from across Azeroth.

Top four sites to visit in Orgrimmar

Grommash Hold

Located in the the heart of Orgrimmar at the Valley of Strength, the Grommash Hold is a towering fortress that served as the former seat of the Warchief of the Horde, and now it is the seat of the Horde Council.

Western Earthshrine

Located in the western part of the city, the Earthshrine is a tranquil oasis amidst the hustle and bustle of Orgrimmar. It serves as a gathering place for the wise and spiritual leaders of the Horde, who come here to meditate, reflect, and connect with the elements.

Ring of Valor

The Ring of Valor is a massive arena that serves as the premier venue for gladiatorial combat in all of Azeroth. Built in honor of the heroic sacrifices made by the Horde's greatest warriors, the Ring of Valor is a symbol of the Horde's martial might and unyielding determination.

The Cleft of Shadows

This mysterious and atmospheric district is home to the Horde's more shadowy citizens. Here, you can witness the more secretive and elusive side of Horde culture and explore the district's many hidden nooks and crannies. Just be careful not to get caught up in any nefarious activities!
